Kathy's Baked Salmon

United States

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb. salmon fillet, divided
  • olive oil
  • seasoned salt
  • dill

Directions:

Pre-heat oven to 350 degrees.

Line a brownie pan or square pan with aluminum foil, shiny side up.

Lay salmon fillets in lined pan, skin side down.

Spread olive oil over salmon fillets.

Sprinkle with seasoned salt.

Sprinkle liberally with dill.

Bake:

Bake uncovered at 350 for 15-20 minutes depending on thickness of fillets.

Serve immediately with accompaniment of your choice such as rice and steamed spinach.

Serves 2-3 people.
